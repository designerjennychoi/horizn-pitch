We are a multi-company treasury autopilot platform. Our agents act as the AI-powered treasury middle layer that manages RWA-backed credit, business payments, and risk — 24/7, in USDC, on-chain.

Unlock working capital
from real-world assets.

Companies deposit tokenized real-world assets — bonds, treasury bills, real estate — as collateral. Our system automatically unlocks USDC working capital against those assets, continuously monitors collateral value and risk, and autonomously adjusts or repays positions as markets move.

Always-on liquidity,
across every account.

We manage liquidity across accounts to ensure businesses always have cash available for payroll, vendors, and operational expenses — automatically, without manual intervention.
